# City Profile and Statistics — Tuguegarao City

## Overview

**Tuguegarao City** is the **capital of Cagayan Province** and a **2nd class component city** in **Region II (Cagayan Valley)**, Philippines. It is the **regional hub** of the Cagayan Valley, serving as a major educational, commercial, and administrative center for the northern Luzon region.

## Economy

Tuguegarao City's economy is driven by a diverse mix of agriculture, retail, education, and services:

- **Agriculture** — Rice, corn, tobacco, rootcrops, vegetables, poultry, hogs, and fishponds serve the region
- **Retail and Commerce** — Central business district with malls, banking, and automotive services
- **Education** — Home to Cagayan State University (CSU), University of Cagayan Valley (UCV), and numerous other higher education institutions
- **Healthcare** — Multiple hospitals and clinics serving the city and surrounding municipalities
- **Emerging Sectors** — Business Process Outsourcing (BPO), food processing, furniture and automotive shops, rice mills, and hollow block manufacturing

### Major Landmarks and Institutions

- St. Peter Metropolitan Cathedral (Tuguegarao Cathedral)

- Tuguegarao East Central School (former city hall site, now a heritage landmark)
