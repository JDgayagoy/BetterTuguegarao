# DTI CMCI Profile — Tuguegarao City

The **Cities and Municipalities Competitiveness Index (CMCI)** is an annual ranking published by the **Department of Trade and Industry (DTI)** that measures how competitive Philippine cities and municipalities are as places to do business and invest.

---

## What Is the CMCI?

The CMCI evaluates local government units (LGUs) across **five pillars**:

| Pillar                    | What It Measures                                                                          |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Economic Dynamism**     | Business registrations, local revenues, employment, investments, and economic activity    |
| **Government Efficiency** | Compliance with national standards, transparency, ease of doing business, public services |
| **Infrastructure**        | Road network, utilities, internet connectivity, transport, and public facilities          |
| **Resiliency**            | Disaster risk reduction, environmental programs, and climate adaptation measures          |
| **Innovation**            | Technology adoption, digital services, research and development, and e-governance         |

---

## Tuguegarao City CMCI Standing

Tuguegarao City has been recognized as one of the **most competitive component cities in the Philippines**, ranking among the **top regionally competitive cities** in Region II (Cagayan Valley). The city's competitiveness is driven by its role as the provincial capital, strong educational institutions, and strategic location.

### Key Competitive Strengths

- **Economic Dynamism** — Strong business registration rates; established commercial district serving the Cagayan Valley region
- **Government Efficiency** — Consistent compliance with Full Disclosure Policy (FDP); streamlined business permit processing through the Business One-Stop Shop (BOSS)
- **Infrastructure** — Road network connecting the region, public markets, and access to major regional highways
- **Resiliency** — Active CDRRMO operations covering 49 barangays; disaster preparedness programs
- **Innovation** — As a regional education hub, expanding e-governance services and digital transformation initiatives

---

## Business One-Stop Shop (BOSS)

As part of the **Ease of Doing Business Act (RA 11032)**, Tuguegarao City operates a **Business One-Stop Shop (BOSS)** that allows entrepreneurs to complete business registration and renewal processes in a single location.

- **Location:** City Hall, Carig Sur, Tuguegarao City, Cagayan 3500
- **Services:** Business permit application, renewal, barangay clearance coordination, BIR coordination, fire safety clearance
