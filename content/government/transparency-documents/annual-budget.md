# Annual Budget — Tuguegarao City

The **Annual Budget** is the appropriations ordinance passed by the **Sangguniang Panlungsod** that authorizes the City Government of Tuguegarao to collect revenues and incur expenditures for the fiscal year.

---

## Budget Sources

| Source                           | Description                                                     |
| -------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **National Tax Allotment (NTA)** | Primary national fund share from the Bureau of Internal Revenue |
| **Local Taxes**                  | Real property tax, business tax, and other local levies         |
| **Non-Tax Revenue**              | Fees, charges, and income from city-owned properties            |
| **Grants and Subsidies**         | Funds from national agencies and external donors                |

---

## Budget Allocation Areas

| Area                    | Description                                                  |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| General Public Services | Administration, legislative, planning, and support offices   |
| Health                  | City Health Office, health centers, and related services     |
| Education (SEF)         | Special Education Fund for school buildings and scholarships |
| Social Services         | CSWDO programs for senior citizens, PWDs, solo parents       |
| Economic Services       | Agriculture, tourism, BPLO, PESO operations                  |
| Infrastructure          | Roads, drainage, public facilities, and capital outlay       |
| Debt Service            | Repayment of authorized loans                                |
| 20% Development Fund    | Earmarked for development projects per NTA rules             |
| 5% DRRMF                | Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Fund                  |
| 1% GAD Fund             | Gender and Development programs                              |

---

## Budget Cycle

| Step                                    | Timeline                        |
| --------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Executive prepares the budget           | July – August                   |
| Mayor submits to Sangguniang Panlungsod | On or before October 16         |
| Sangguniang deliberates and enacts      | October – December              |
| Budget takes effect                     | January 1 of the following year |
