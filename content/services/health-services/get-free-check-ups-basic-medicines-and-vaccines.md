# Get Free Check-ups, Basic Medicines, and Vaccines — Tuguegarao City

The Tuguegarao City Health Office (CHO) and barangay health centers offer free **medical consultations, basic medicines, and immunization** for all residents.

---

## 1. Free Medical Check-ups

| Service              | Where                          | Schedule                                    | Requirements                                              |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| General consultation | City / Barangay Health Centers | Mon–Fri, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM (varies by center) | Individual Treatment Record (ITR) — issued on first visit |

---

## 2. Free Basic Medicines

| Service                        | Where                                                   | Schedule                 | Requirements                                                              |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Free basic medicines           | City Health Office (CHO) pharmacy                       | Mon–Fri, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM | Doctor's prescription and/or referral from hospital/clinic                |
| Medicines for indigent seniors | Partner pharmacies (confirm current locations with CHO) | Pharmacy hours           | Must be an indigent senior citizen and bona fide Tuguegarao City resident |

---

## 3. Free Vaccines & Immunization

| Beneficiary       | Vaccine / Service                                        | Where                        | Schedule                              | Requirements                                          |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Children          | BCG, Pentavalent, OPV/IPV, MMR, Hepatitis B, PCV13 (EPI) | Barangay Health Centers      | Typically Wednesdays, 8:00 AM–4:00 PM | ECCD card (first-timers can get one onsite)           |
| Pregnant women    | Tetanus Toxoid vaccine + supplements                     | Barangay Health Centers      | During prenatal services              | Proof of pregnancy / prenatal record                  |
| Animal bite cases | Anti-rabies vaccine (first dose free)                    | Animal Bite Treatment Center | Clinic hours                          | Proof of Tuguegarao City residency (e.g., voter's ID) |

---

## How to Avail

1. **Check-ups** → Go to your Barangay Health Center or City Health Office. First-timers will be issued an ITR.
2. **Medicines** → Bring a doctor's prescription or referral; submit at the CHO pharmacy window.
3. **Child immunization** → Visit your barangay health center on immunization day with your child's ECCD card.
4. **Pregnancy services** → Attend prenatal visits at the barangay health center for free vaccines and supplements.
5. **Anti-rabies** → Report immediately to the Animal Bite Treatment Center; bring proof of residency.
6. **Senior citizen medicines** → Visit participating partner pharmacies; ask the CHO for current program guidelines.
